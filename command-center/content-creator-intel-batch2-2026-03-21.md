## 📱 INSTAGRAM DM SALES FRAMEWORK

### The Two-Engine Model
- **Engine 1: Magnet** — Content that attracts perfect buyers (PSL framework)
- **Engine 2: Money** — DM conversations that convert

### DM Sales Process
1. **Open:** Ask a qualifying question when someone follows
2. **Qualify:** "Are you here for content or looking for help with X?"
3. **Diagnose:** Be a doctor — identify their real pain, don't just validate
4. **Prescribe:** Simple, clear offer that addresses their specific challenge
5. **Close:** "Ready? Here's the link" — don't overcomplicate

### Content Strategy
- PSL: Point → Story → Lesson (one per day, 100 days)
- Use ChatGPT: "Create 25 PSL content ideas for [niche] around [problem]"
- Pre-validate: Find people with max audiences making similar content
- Algorithm is interest-based now, not follower-based — zero followers can go viral

- "$6M sold chatting with strangers on Instagram"
- "15.5M average views after 90 days of posting"
- "1,000 followers can generate $1M/year"
