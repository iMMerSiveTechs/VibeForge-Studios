## PHASE 3: FIRST AUDIT — Fit Assessment

### NEW MODULES TO CREATE (4)

#### Module 10: Identity Upgrade Engine
- **Frameworks:** F1 (5 Mindset Shifts), F6 (Upgrade Loop), F5 (AI Vision Board)
- **Why it fits:** JayTee is in a massive identity transformation. From consumer to builder, from scattered to systematic. Every framework in Founder Companion works better when the PERSON is evolving.
- **Why it doesn't fit (risk):** Could become too "self-help" and distract from shipping product. Must be tied to ACTION, not reflection.
- **Mitigation:** Every identity exercise MUST end with "and here's what you build/ship because of this shift."

#### Module 11: Leverage Architect
- **Frameworks:** F2 (5 Business Shifts), F18 (Data Moats), F19 (Autonomous Back Office), F20 (Leverage Maps), F22 (AI-First Workflows)
- **Why it fits:** JayTee is literally building a multi-agent AI company. This IS the operational playbook.
- **Why it doesn't fit (risk):** Could be too abstract/theoretical when he needs to ship a $4.99 app first.
- **Mitigation:** Tier it — "Pre-revenue leverage" (what applies now) vs "Post-revenue leverage" (what applies at $5K MRR+).

#### Module 12: Audience Engine
- **Frameworks:** F13 (PSL), F14 (Two-Engine Model), F21 (Distribution Advantage), F11 (Instagram Automation)
- **Why it fits:** Revenue = customers. Customers = distribution. Zero-cost distribution = content + DM sales. This is the missing link between "build product" and "make money."
- **Why it doesn't fit (risk):** Could pull focus from product development. Content creation is a time sink.
- **Mitigation:** PSL is ONE post per day. Not a content empire. Keep it minimal and consistent.

#### Module 13: Goal Systems Builder (aka "5-Year Architect")
- **Frameworks:** F3 (SYSTEMS), F4 (SMART Goals), F5 (Vision Board), F6 (Upgrade Loop)
- **Why it fits:** No goal cascading system exists. JayTee set "5x monthly costs" as a target but has no structured breakdown to weekly actions.
- **Why it doesn't fit (risk):** Over-planning. JayTee's strength is action, not planning. Too much goal architecture could slow him down.
- **Mitigation:** Keep it lightweight. 5-year → this quarter → this week. No daily granularity unless requested.
