## 💼 BUSINESS STRATEGY INTEL (Dan Martell / Others)

### 5 Shifts Coming in AI Era

**1. Org Charts → Leverage Teams**
- One person + AI can do the work of 10
- "Infinite SDR" — AI does outbound, qualifies inbound, personalizes emails, manages calendar
- Marketing: AI identifies content topics, creates outlines, generates campaigns
- **Hire an AI Ops person** — their job is giving the team time back

**2. Doer → Director**
- 10/80/10 Rule: 10% ideation (you), 80% execution (AI), 10% taste/polish (you)
- Switch from "push prompting" (telling AI how) to "pull prompting" (telling AI WHAT, let it ask questions)
- Build AI-first workflows for every bottleneck

**3. Features → Data Moat**
- AI builds features faster than humans — features aren't the moat
- Proprietary data IS the moat
- Collect customer preferences, save them, use for personalization
- Clean data first (garbage in = garbage out)
- Use AI to analyze data and suggest next steps

**4. Departments → Autonomous Back Office**
- Finance, HR, Legal → AI policy-driven agents
- Connect financial systems → AI monitors, audits, reports in real-time
- "Exceptions deserve people, patterns deserve code"

**5. Development → Distribution Advantage**
- Code is commoditized (12-year-olds build apps with AI)
- Distribution (brand, audience, reach) is the moat
- Build one owned channel (email, community, social)
- Pre-sell before building
- Partner with people who have existing audiences

### The Mindset Framework
1. **Trainer:** Use AI to learn faster, not to think for you
2. **Taste:** Develop excellence recognition through immersion
3. **Vision:** Block time for deep thinking, use AI to pressure-test assumptions
4. **Care:** Genuine care for people = unfakeable competitive advantage
5. **Director:** Be the 10% on each end, let AI handle the 80% in the middle
